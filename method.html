Method

Art is one expression of the human emotion. My paintings have two themes: Nostalgia and happy feelings using color, candy, and comics as well as toys as the vehicles for the expression of these feelings.


Photorealism is the technique, so anyone looking at my work understands the subject and can relate to it easily. The warm feelings of seeing a candy or toy usually evokes a smile. One does not have to be "taught" about the work in order to appreciate it. I always use photography in setting up the subject. Enjoy.
